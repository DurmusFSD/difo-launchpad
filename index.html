    Protecting the Principal Capital of the Investors

    Difo Launchpad has specially designed a buyback protection program for investors participating in its released IDOs to safeguard their principal capital. Through this buyback protection program, investors are shielded with 100% protection against any FUD (fear, uncertainty, doubt) events.

    Buyback Protection Program

    The grant program was established by the Difo Finance Foundation to discover and support developers creating dApps using Difo Finance's multi-chain middleware technology, as well as various dApps that can contribute to the growth of the Difo Finance ecosystem.

    90% Protection Guarantee

    Difo Launchpad will ensure the protection of 90% of the principal capital of all investors participating in the IDOs or public token sales of the projects it launches through a comprehensive buyback protection policy for a period of 6 months.

    Price Guarantee on Locked Tokens

    Difo Launchpad will ensure the token price to be above the IDO or public token sale price at the time of 100% of the token distribution to all of its investors is completed. If the target price is not met, the locked tokens below the IDO price on the day of unlocking will be repurchased by Difo Launchpad in USDT, USDC, or BUSD equal to the sale price.

    Authorization Plan Compliance

    In the event of project failures, or any unforeseen events that may leave investors at risk, an authorization plan will go into force. Difo Launchpad will begin to protect the investors' principal only in cases where the project has a violation in its operation or fails to meet its objectives. The plan will come into effect if any such adverse situations arise.

    Projects with malicious functions in their smart contracts are not acceptable.

    Difo Launchpad will ensure that the project's smart contracts do not have any malicious functions such as high gas, rug pull, proxy contracts, unexpected ownership changes, or other possible forms of manipulation, mining, or other types that allow malicious tax systems.
